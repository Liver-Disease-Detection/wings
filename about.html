Introduction

Welcome to our Liver Disease Prediction platform! We aim to provide information about liver diseases and how we use predictive models to assess the risk of liver disease in individuals.

What is Liver Disease?

Liver disease refers to a range of conditions that affect the liver's structure and function. It can be caused by various factors, including viral infections, alcohol abuse, obesity, and more. Liver diseases can be mild or severe and may lead to serious health complications if left untreated.

Prediction Methodology

Our liver disease prediction model uses machine learning algorithms to analyze various factors such as age, gender, medical history, and lifestyle choices to assess an individual's risk of developing liver disease. The model is based on extensive research and data analysis to provide accurate predictions.

Common Risk Factors

  • Excessive alcohol consumption
  • Obesity and unhealthy diet
  • Hepatitis infection
  • Family history of liver disease
  • Diabetes
